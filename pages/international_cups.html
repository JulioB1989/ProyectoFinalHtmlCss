International Cups

Uefa Champions League

The UEFA Champions League (abbreviated as UCL) is an annual club football competition organised by the Union of European Football Associations (UEFA) and contested by top-division European clubs, deciding the competition winners through a round robin group stage to qualify for a double-legged knockout format, and a single leg final.

American Liberators Cup

The CONMEBOL Libertadores, also known as the Copa Libertadores de América (Portuguese: Copa Libertadores da América), is an annual international club football competition organized by CONMEBOL since 1960. It is the highest level of competition in South American club football. The tournament is named after the Libertadores".

Copa Libertadores 2021

Four eight-team pots

They were drawn according to their position in the CONMEBOL club ranking.

  • In Pot 1

    • were the last champions, Palmeiras, and the best placed teams up to 1 February this year: River, Boca, Palmeiras, Nacional of Uruguay, Flamengo, Cerro Porteno, Olimpia and Sao Paulo.

  • In Pot 2

    • were Sudamericana 2020 champions Defensa y Justicia, Racing, Internacional de Porto Alegre, Atlético Mineiro, Santa Fe, Liga de Quito, Universidad Católica and Barcelona of Ecuador.

  • Drawn in Pot 3

    • were Velez, Argentinos Juniors, Sporting Cristal, America de Cali, Fluminense, The Strongest, Universitario and Deportivo Tachira.

  • Pot 4

    • was made up of Deportivo La Guaira, Unión La Calera, Always Ready, Rentistas, Santos, Independiente del Valle, Atlético Nacional and Junior.
